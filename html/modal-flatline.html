Nerf Rayven Bodykit

A bodykit for the Nerf Rayven blaster to convert it into the Flatline Reactive from Apex Legends

I met one of my best friends in college over video games, specifically Apex Legends. Over COVID, we played hours of Apex and bonded over it. The Flatline rifle was always his favorite gun in the game, specifically its Reactive skin. For his birthday, I wanted to do something special for him, and I thought it would be so cool to create a firing version of the Flatline Reactive skin.

Image of the Modded Blaster

Objective:
  • Create a firing Nerf blaster in time for my friend's birthday using a pre-existing Nerf blaster
Challenges:
  • Extracting a game asset and converting it into a usable model in Solidworks
  • Modelling using BREP bodies in Solidworks
  • Designing easy to assemble features to assemble the blaster together
  • Marketing the blaster on Makerworld
Results:
  • Submission to Makerworld

Takeaways:
  • Game assets are tricky to work with in Solidworks because their models are optimized for games (Blender is better for this)
  • Timelines really push you and don't let perfectionism consume you
